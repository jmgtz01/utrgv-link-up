About UTRGV Link-Up

Your all-in-one companion for navigating campus resources. UTRGV Link-Up bridges the gap between students and the tools they need to succeed.

Our Mission

UTRGV Link-Up was created to streamline the student experience. We know that finding a computer during finals week, borrowing a camera for a project, or just finding something to do on campus can be a hassle. Our platform brings these essential services together in one easy-to-use application, empowering Vaqueros to make the most of their time on campus.

Reserve a Computer

Stop wandering the halls looking for an open seat. View real-time availability in computer labs across campus and reserve your workstation in advance to guarantee your spot.

Media Equipment

Need a DSLR for photography class or a laptop for the weekend? Browse the library's inventory and check out media equipment directly through the app, hassle-free.

Campus Events

Never miss out on the action. View and attend UTRGV events ranging from career fairs to student life gatherings. RSVP with a single click and stay connected.
